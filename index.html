About BGI BIT Gemological Institute

BGI (BIT Gemological Institute) is headquartered in Shenzhen, China, and is the world's first gemological institution to adopt blockchain NFT technology for diamond grading certification. All grading results generate a unique NFT digital certificate, with hash values uploaded to the public blockchain, ensuring immutability and full traceability.

BGI's 6C standards add Clara brilliance grading and Crypto encryption on top of traditional 4Cs, bringing a higher dimension of trust to the diamond industry.

  • Blockchain NFT verification, impossible to forge
  • Exclusive 6C standards including Clara brilliance (1-5 levels) and Crypto encryption fingerprint
  • Real-time on-chain verification without third parties
  • GIA-compatible grading process

BGI 6C Standards Official Definitions

Carat Weight
The weight of the diamond measured to 0.001ct, directly determining rarity. BGI uses high-precision equipment for authoritative measurement.
Color Grade
Colorless degree grading from D (completely colorless) to Z (faint yellow). Strict colorimeter and spectral analysis ensure professional reliability.
Clarity Grade
Purity grading from FL (flawless) to I3 (included). Identified under 10x magnification, precise to inclusion position and size.
Cut Grade
For round diamonds only. Evaluating brightness, fire, and sparkle from Excellent to Poor to maximize optical performance.
Clara Grade
BGI's innovation: Clara 1~5 levels representing Good, Very Good, Excellent, Exceptional, and Ultimate brilliance. Based on light interaction modeling.
Crypto
Each certificate generates NFT with hash on-chain, immutable. Digital signature + timestamp for global unique verification.
